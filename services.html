Insurance & Costs

Private Health Insurance

Most services are covered by private health insurance in Germany. Coverage typically ranges from 75-90% depending on your specific plan and the services provided.

Laboratory Costs

Laboratory tests are billed separately based on the laboratory's workload. These costs are generally almost entirely reimbursable by private health insurance companies.

Transparent Pricing

You'll always know the expected costs before beginning treatment. I provide detailed cost estimates and help you understand what your insurance will likely cover.
