Understanding Costs & Insurance

You'll always know the expected costs before beginning treatment. I provide detailed cost estimates and help you understand what your insurance will likely cover.

Private Health Insurance

All programs are eligible for private health insurance reimbursement in Germany. Coverage rates typically range from 75-90% depending on your specific plan and the services included in your program.

Laboratory Costs

Laboratory tests are billed separately based on the laboratory's actual costs. These are generally almost entirely covered by private health insurance. You'll receive detailed cost estimates before any testing is performed.

Online Sessions & Programs

For online sessions and programs, a brief consultation call can be scheduled before payment. After we determine the right approach for you, you'll receive a PayPal.me link for convenient payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral from my general practitioner?

No referral is needed. You can book directly with our practice for any of our services or programs.

How long are appointments?

Initial consultations typically last 60-90 minutes. Follow-up appointments are usually 30-45 minutes. I believe in taking the time needed to truly understand your situation and answer all your questions.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, any recent medical records or test results, a list of current medications, and any questions you'd like to discuss.

Do you accept private health insurance?

Yes, all services are eligible for reimbursement by private health insurance in Germany. Coverage rates typically range from 75-90% depending on your plan.

Can I book online or video consultations?

Yes, I offer both in-person and online consultations via video. This is particularly convenient for follow-up appointments or if you're unable to visit the practice in person.

What languages do you speak?

Consultations are available in German and English.
