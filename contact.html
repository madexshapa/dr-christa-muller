Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?

No, you can book directly with us. A referral from your GP is not required for gynecological care.

How long is an initial appointment?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes, giving us adequate time to understand your situation and develop a treatment plan together.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, any relevant medical records, a list of current medications, and any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we are expanding to offer online consultations for follow-up appointments and certain types of consultations. Contact us for more information.

Can I switch to a different program later?

Absolutely. We can adjust your care plan at any time based on your evolving needs and goals. Your care is always personalized.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Most appointments are available within 1-2 weeks. For urgent concerns, please call the practice directly and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
