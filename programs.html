Menopause Care Programs

Every woman's experience of menopause is unique. That's why I offer three distinct program levels, each designed to meet you where you are and provide exactly the support you need.

Program 1

Breathe a Sigh of Relief, There is Hope

Immediate relief and guidance for physical symptoms

Who is this program for?

This program is ideal for women who are primarily experiencing physical symptoms of menopause - hot flashes, sleep disturbances, fatigue, or other discomforts - and need quick, effective medical support to restore balance and well-being.

If you're looking to understand what's happening in your body and find relief through proven medical interventions, this focused program provides exactly that.

What you'll receive

A comprehensive medical assessment followed by individualized treatment designed to alleviate your symptoms. We'll work together to understand your specific hormonal needs and develop a treatment approach that brings you relief.

Your Journey

  • 1st Appointment: Initial Consultation

    Approximately 30 minutes. Comprehensive discussion of your symptoms, medical history, and health goals. Blood draw if necessary.

  • 2nd Appointment: Physical Examination & Treatment Plan

    Physical gynecological examination and detailed consultation. Review of any lab results. Together, we decide on a therapeutic approach (medication if necessary) and you receive complete instructions on how to proceed.

  • 3rd Appointment: Follow-up Evaluation

    After approximately 6-8 weeks. Physical gynecological examination and detailed consultation to discuss your progress and adjust treatment as needed.

Program 2 - Most Popular

Shining... Your Strong Tomorrow

Comprehensive body and mind support

Who is this program for?

This program is designed for women who recognize that menopause is more than just physical symptoms. It's for those who want to address the deeper questions that arise during this transition - questions about identity, purpose, intimacy, and the path forward.

If you're experiencing both physical symptoms and emotional challenges, if you're ready to not just "get through" menopause but to use this time for personal growth and renewal, this comprehensive program provides the support you need.

What you'll receive

A holistic approach that addresses your physical symptoms through medical treatment while also providing psychological support, nutritional guidance, and lifestyle counseling. This is about helping you rediscover yourself and step into this new phase of life with strength and clarity.

Your Journey

  • 1st Appointment: Comprehensive Initial Consultation

    30-45 minutes. In-depth discussion of physical symptoms, emotional state, lifestyle, and goals. Blood draw if necessary.

  • 2nd Appointment: Physical Examination & Detailed Planning

    Physical gynecological examination and extended consultation. Further tests may be required (nutritional counseling, glucose tolerance test, intolerance or microbiome diagnostics). Together, we develop your comprehensive treatment approach.

  • 3rd & 4th Appointments: Nutrition and Lifestyle Counseling

    Two 40-minute sessions scheduled individually. Personalized nutritional guidance, metabolic support, and lifestyle optimization strategies.

  • 5th Appointment: Follow-up Evaluation

    After approximately 6-8 weeks. Physical gynecological examination with detailed consultation to discuss progress and refine your treatment plan.

  • Psychological & Philosophical Support

    2-3 sessions of 45 minutes each. Exploration of identity, purpose, and personal growth during this transformative time.

Program 3

Deep Transformation

Complete holistic care over 6 months

Who is this program for?

This comprehensive program is for women who are ready to explore menopause on all levels - physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual. It's for those who recognize this transition as a profound opportunity for transformation and want support every step of the way.

If you feel that life is asking you to finally take care of yourself on all levels, if you want to replenish your energy reserves and emerge from this experience stronger, clearer, and more vibrant than ever, this is your program.

What you'll receive

A complete 6-month journey encompassing medical care, psychological support, nutritional optimization, lifestyle coaching, infusion therapy, hormone-based cosmetics, and continuous support. Everything you need, from a single trusted source, to transform this challenging time into your most empowering chapter yet.

Your 6-Month Journey

  • 1st Appointment: Comprehensive Assessment

    30-45 minutes. Complete evaluation of all aspects of your health, symptoms, goals, and vision for this transformation. Blood draw if necessary.

  • 2nd Appointment: Physical Examination & Treatment Planning

    Physical gynecological examination and detailed consultation. Comprehensive diagnostic testing (nutritional counseling, glucose tolerance test, intolerance or microbiome diagnostics). We determine all next steps together.

  • 3rd & 4th Appointments: Nutrition and Lifestyle Counseling

    Two appointments 4 weeks apart. Personalized nutrition plans, metabolic optimization, and lifestyle transformation strategies.

  • 5th Appointment: Psychological and Philosophical Support

    5 sessions of 45 minutes each. Mental strengthening with focus on positive transformation. Exploring your strengths, addressing challenges, and discovering your renewed sense of purpose.

  • 6th Appointment: Continuous Support

    Throughout your 6-month journey, you have access to support via email or WhatsApp for questions, concerns, or guidance.

  • 7th Appointment: Hormone Cosmetic Consultation

    If desired, individualized treatment plan for skin rejuvenation and hormone-based cosmetic care.

  • 8th Appointment: Infusion Therapy

    6 sessions of medical anti-stress therapy with vitamin booster infusions to restore energy and vitality.

  • 9th Appointment: Final Consultation

    Comprehensive review of your transformation, celebration of progress, and planning for continued wellness.

Understanding Costs & Insurance

You'll always know the expected costs before beginning treatment. I provide detailed cost estimates and help you understand what your insurance will likely cover.

Private Health Insurance

All programs are eligible for private health insurance reimbursement in Germany. Coverage rates typically range from 75-90% depending on your specific plan and the services included in your program.

Laboratory Costs

Laboratory tests are billed separately based on the laboratory's actual costs. These are generally almost entirely covered by private health insurance. You'll receive detailed cost estimates before any testing is performed.

Online Sessions & Programs

For online sessions and programs, a brief consultation call can be scheduled before payment. After we determine the right approach for you, you'll receive a PayPal.me link for convenient payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right program for me?

During your initial consultation, we'll discuss your symptoms, goals, and preferences to determine which program best fits your needs. You can also schedule a brief consultation call before booking to discuss which program might be right for you.

Can I switch programs if needed?

Yes, absolutely. Your program can be adjusted based on how you respond to treatment and any changes in your needs or goals throughout your journey.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed. You can book directly for any of our programs.

Are programs covered by insurance?

Yes, all programs are eligible for private health insurance reimbursement in Germany. Coverage rates typically range from 75-90% depending on your plan and the services included in your program.

How long do the programs take?

The Quick Start program typically spans 2-3 months, the Comprehensive Program runs 3-4 months, and the Deep Transformation program is a complete 6-month journey. Each timeline is flexible and can be adjusted to your needs.

Can I do programs online or must I come in person?

Many appointments can be conducted online via video consultation, though some physical examinations and initial consultations are better done in person. We'll create a schedule that balances convenience with comprehensive care.
